Privacy Policy

Last updated: July, 2025
1. Information We Collect Expense Tracker - Budget Manager is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience. The app may collect your device information, app usage statistics, and any feedback you choose to submit through the feedback form.
2. How We Use Your Information We use the collected information solely to improve Expense Tracker - Budget Manager and provide better user experience. Your personal financial data (transactions, categories, etc.) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.
3. Data Storage and Security All your financial data, including transactions, categories, and user profile information, is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not have access to your personal financial information. Your data remains private and under your control.
4. Third-Party Services Expense Tracker - Budget Manager may use third-party services for analytics, feedback collection, and advertising. These services are carefully selected and only collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app and provide relevant advertisements. No personal financial information is ever shared with these services.
5. Advertising Expense Tracker - Budget Manager displays advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads may collect certain information such as your device identifier, IP address, and general location to provide relevant advertisements. AdMob's use of this information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not share your personal financial data with advertisers.
6. Data Retention Your financial data is retained on your device for as long as you use Expense Tracker - Budget Manager. If you uninstall the app, your data will be removed from your device. Feedback submitted through the app is retained for support and improvement purposes.
7. Your Rights You have complete control over your data. You can export, modify, or delete your financial information at any time through the app. You can also request deletion of any feedback you've submitted by contacting us through the feedback form.
8. Children's Privacy Expense Tracker - Budget Manager is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of Expense Tracker - Budget Manager after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
10. Contact Us If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please use the feedback form within Expense Tracker - Budget Manager. We are committed to protecting your privacy and will respond to your inquiries promptly.
Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency in our data practices.
